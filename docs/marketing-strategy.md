**"Jedine digitalne pozivnice u Srbiji sa 10 potpuno različitih stilova"**

## PRICING STRATEGY

### Recommended Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Price | Target | Features |
|------|-------|--------|----------|
| **BESPLATNO** | €0 | Lead generation | 1 basic layout, watermark, 30-day preview |
| **STARTER** | €39 | Budget couples | 3 layouts, 5 themes, RSVP, 6-month hosting |
| **STANDARD** | €59 | Most couples | All layouts, all themes, RSVP+map, 12-month hosting |
| **PREMIUM** | €89 | Demanding couples | Everything + custom domain + unlimited hosting |

### Pricing Psychology

1. **€39 Starter** - Undercuts sajtzavencanje.rs (€59) by 34%
2. **€59 Standard** - Same as competitor's basic, but MORE features
3. **€89 Premium** - Below competitor's €109, positions as value

### Launch Promotion Ideas
- **"Prvih 50 parova - 50% popusta"** - Build reviews fast
- **"Poklon za verenicu"** - Partner discount with jewelry stores

1. **Instagram** (Primary) - Visual platform, wedding audience

3. **Pinterest** (Tertiary) - Wedding planning boards
