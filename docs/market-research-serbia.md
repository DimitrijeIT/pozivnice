## MARKET GAPS & OPPORTUNITIES

### What competitors DON'T offer:

1. **Multiple unique layouts** - Everyone offers same scrolling format. You have 10 different:
   - Classic scroll
   - Envelope opening
   - Storybook chapters
   - Newspaper gazette
   - Cinema premiere
   - Passport booklet
   - Concert poster
   - Magazine editorial
   - Vintage telegram
   - Love letter

2. **Instant generation** - Competitors take 1-3 days, you can be instant

3. **Modern design trends** - Most Serbian competitors have outdated designs (floral, lace from 2015)

4. **Theme variety** - Most offer 5-15 themes, you have 20+ across layouts

5. **No time-limited hosting** - Competitors limit to 6-12 months

### 2. Instagram Strategy

- Post **daily** with Reels showing unique layouts
- Use hashtags: #pozivnicezavencanje #svadba #vencanje #pozivnice #digitalnepozivnice
